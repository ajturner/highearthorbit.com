highearthorbit.com now supports the LOC information in its DNS record as discussed in the LJ Article and discussed here. To query the information, go to a command line on a suitable *nix machine, or with cygwin, and type:

host -t LOC highearthorbit.com

You can try out a LOC mapping service provided by Christopher Davis.
