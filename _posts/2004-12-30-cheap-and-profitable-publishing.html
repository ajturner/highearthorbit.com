I read about Lulu Publishing Service over at Greg Smith's Blog (via Smart Home Hacks). They offer pretty cheap publishing (books at $4.53 + $0.15/color page +$0.02/bw page). and you can even add a "profit" you get if your book (calendar, cd, etc.) sells.
