So I'm a long-time Primus fan. Don't hold it against me. I blame the influences of several childhood friends. I started out not being able to listen to them, and then slowly got into the groove. I got to see them perform at Tower Theater in Philadelphia back somewhere around 1995, and haven't purchased an album since 'Tales From the Punchbowl'.

Well, driving past the Royal Oak Theater I saw "Colonel Claypool - Sept 27". It's actually Colonel Claypool's Bucket of Bernie Brains.

The Theater is right next to work, so I have to go. I started looking around and have seen a bunch of good stuff, including Primus Live Downloads.
