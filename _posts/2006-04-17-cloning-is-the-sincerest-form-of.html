ParkPlace is a clone of Amazon's S3 online web storage. Basically, you can use all the cool tools showing up to have your own S3-like online storage service and save yourself some mulah (ybmv).

Some Cool S3 Tools

  • Backup Flickr photos
  • BitBucket arbitrary programmatic storage
  • Serving media
  • Perl S3 interface
