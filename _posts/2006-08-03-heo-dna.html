Wonder what your site's DNA looks like? Now you can see it rendered as DNA by using Web2DNA. It's a bookmarklet (drop into your bookmarks toolbar) that pulls apart the HTML to display a DNA-like representation based on H-tags, tables, and formatting.

Though with a blog, the DNA is very volatile, and goes through many mutations, depending on the day.
