DashSaver has been released in the wild. Get it while it's still hot. well... ok, get it while it's still on the front page of VersionTracker? You've got 30 seconds, go!

As I've mentioned before, it runs Dashboard as your screensaver on Mac OS X Tiger or higher (if you're from the future, this version of DashSaver doesn't work on 10.6 (TabbyCat) or 11.0 (Platypus)). If you don't have Tiger (or a Mac), then fer goodness sakes, get one!
