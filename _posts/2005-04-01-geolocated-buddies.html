BuddySpace is a Java application which allows optional maps for geographical & office-plan visualizations in addition to standard 'buddy lists'". Looks like a really good implementation of locating buddies on maps. This could be the world, a region, or even just an office. This would be great for when you're trying to locate some wandering co-worker, or even find out where a friend is chatting you from.
