Haha - it's alive!

SpyTrack now supports displaying an ICBM RSS Module feed. Such as Quakes.

The tgz binary file is available here. After starting up the app, go the Preferences and change the "Online" datasource to "ICBM" and use one of the following datasources:

  • This weblog: http://marchhare.dyndns.org/wp-rss2.php
  • M > 2.5 worldwide earthquakes, past 7 days: http://earthquake.usgs.gov/recenteqsww/eqs7day-M2.5.xml
  • 3D Artists around the world: http://www.2112fx.com/rss.xml
  • UK Bloggers: http://philwilson.org/ukbloggermap/rss.xml
  • Chesapeake Bay Tide Stations: http://www.kayaktrips.net/geo/xtide/rss.php
  • Mikel Maron: GeoPhotoBlog: http://brainoff.com/geophotoblog/rss.xml

I'll post more as I find them.
