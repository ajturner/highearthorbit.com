Chippy has a good post about deep geotagging of videos in MotionBox.

Its like triple-tagging in Flickr, but with deep tagging you can actually point to sub-sections of the video. He's also pointed out that it's useful to add tags like "facingnorth" - something Flickr geotagging has so far ignored.
