BlogMapper is a website with an online app that pulls the geo:lat/lon from a blogs header and displays it on a map. It uses Flash, which is pretty, but also a little annoying for embedding a map image on a frontpage of a site, or a stand-alone app. The app uses the RDFMapper.

In-Duce also talks about Geotags in Exif Headers. Of course, they're lucky enough to live in Japan, where apparently a Yen a dozen, or somesuch.
